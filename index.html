Return Policy

Our goal is that you will always love your Lillians purchases. We take pride in the quality of our merchandise. If you are not completely satisfied or encounter any problems with your purchase please let us know.

Lillians will happily exchange or give in-store credit for all merchandise returned unused/unworn within 30 days of purchase. Returns must be accompanied by a receipt. No cash refunds. All clearance sales are final.
